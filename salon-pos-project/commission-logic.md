# Commission Calculation Logic

Salon staff earn commissions based on services performed.

Commission is calculated automatically using POS transaction data.

---

## Commission Components

Base Commission

Each service has a predefined commission percentage.

Tip Commission

Tips received through POS are distributed to employees.

Total Commission

Total commission is calculated using:

Service Commission + Tip Commission

---

## Reports

Two main reports were created:

Sales Commission Report

Shows total sales and commission earned by employees.

Employee Idle Time Report

Calculates time between services to measure productivity.
