# POS Features

## Shift Management

Custom Opening Shift and Closing Shift DocTypes were created.

Opening shift records:

• Cash opening amount  
• Cashier

Closing shift calculates:

• Expected amount  
• Actual amount  
• Variance

---

## Employee Service Tagging

Each POS item can be assigned to a specific employee.

This allows accurate tracking of services performed by staff.

---

## Employee Activity Tracking

When services are processed through POS:

• Activity logs are created  
• Service start and completion times are recorded

These logs are used for productivity reports.
